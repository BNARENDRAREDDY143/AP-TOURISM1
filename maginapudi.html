Manginapudi Beach

Manginapudi Beach is located near Machilipatnam in Krishna district, Andhra Pradesh. Known for its black soil and calm waters, the beach is a popular weekend getaway with cultural significance and spiritual attractions nearby.

Highlights

Black sand beach, shallow waters for safe swimming, ideal for picnics, nearby temples like Panduranga Swamy Temple.

Accommodation

  • Government Haritha Resort
  • Hotels and lodges available in Machilipatnam (10 km away)
