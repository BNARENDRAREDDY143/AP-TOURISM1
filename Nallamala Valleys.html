Nallamala Valleys

Nestled in the Eastern Ghats, the Nallamala Valleys are a biodiverse and scenic region known for their dense forests, waterfalls, and spiritual retreats.

Highlights

Srisailam Temple, Nagarjunasagar-Srisailam Tiger Reserve, Mallela Theertham waterfall, forest treks, rich flora and fauna

Accommodation

  • Haritha Resort Srisailam (₹1,500-₹3,000)
  • Forest Guest Houses (permit-based)
  • Budget Lodges in Dornala
