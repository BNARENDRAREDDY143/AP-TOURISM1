Kondaveedu Fort

Located in Guntur district, Kondaveedu Fort is a majestic hilltop fort known for its panoramic views, ancient ruins, and historical significance. Built during the 14th century by the Reddy kings, the fort offers scenic hiking trails and a glimpse into medieval South Indian architecture.

Highlights

Hilltop ruins, panoramic views, trekking trails, historical monuments, fort walls, and temple ruins.
