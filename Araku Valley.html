Araku Valley

A picturesque valley nestled in the Eastern Ghats, Araku is known for its scenic coffee plantations, tribal culture, and pleasant climate, making it a favorite hill station in Andhra Pradesh.

Highlights

Borra Caves, Tribal Museum, Katiki Waterfalls, Coffee Museum, Padmapuram Gardens, and the scenic train ride from Vizag.

Best Time to Visit

October to March for cool weather and lush green views.

Accommodation

  • Haritha Valley Resort (₹2,000–₹3,000)
  • Ushodaya Resort (₹1,500–₹2,500)
  • Private Homestays (₹800–₹1,500)
