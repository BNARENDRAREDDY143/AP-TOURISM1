Tirupati

The spiritual capital of Andhra Pradesh, home to the famous Sri Venkateswara Temple atop Tirumala Hills, attracting millions of devotees annually.

History

Tirupati dates back to 300 AD, built by Tamil king Thondaimaan. The temple has been a pilgrimage site for centuries, mentioned in ancient texts like the Puranas.

Highlights

Brahmotsavam festival (September), Talakona Waterfalls, Sri Govindaraja Swamy Temple, Srivari Mettu trekking path
