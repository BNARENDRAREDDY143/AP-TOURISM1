Srisailam

Srisailam is a sacred hill town in Andhra Pradesh, famous for the Mallikarjuna Swamy Temple, one of the 12 Jyotirlingas dedicated to Lord Shiva.

History

The temple has mythological significance dating back to the epics. It is believed that Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati reside here as Mallikarjuna and Bhramaramba.

Highlights

Visit Mallikarjuna Jyotirlinga, Bhramaramba Devi Temple, Srisailam Dam, and enjoy boat rides to Akkamahadevi Caves through Krishna River gorge.

Accommodation

  • Haritha Srisailam (₹2,000-₹3,000) – State tourism guest house
  • Srisaila Devasthanam Cottages (₹500-₹1,500) – Budget rooms for pilgrims
  • Private Hotels (₹1,000-₹2,500) – Available near temple complex
