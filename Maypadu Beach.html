Maypadu Beach

Maypadu Beach is a serene coastal destination in SPSR Nellore district of Andhra Pradesh. Known for its clean shores, minimal crowds, and peaceful environment, it's a hidden gem for beach lovers.

Highlights

Quiet coastline, ideal for relaxation, AP Tourism Haritha Resort available, great for sunrise and sunset views

Accommodation

  • Haritha Beach Resort (₹2,000–₹4,000)
  • Guesthouses and lodges available in nearby Nellore town
