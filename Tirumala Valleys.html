Tirumala Valleys

The Tirumala Valleys, surrounding the sacred Tirumala hills, are a serene and forested landscape that offers tranquility, divine energy, and a breathtaking natural escape for devotees and trekkers alike.

Highlights

Srivari Mettu & Alipiri trekking paths, Papavinasanam waterfalls, scenic ghat roads, and lush forest views across the Seshachalam range.

Best Time to Visit

October to March for cool weather and misty valley views.

Nearby Attractions

Sri Venkateswara Temple, Akasha Ganga, Talakona Falls, Deer Park
