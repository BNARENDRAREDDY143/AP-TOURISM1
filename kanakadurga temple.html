Kanaka Durga Temple

Located on the Indrakeeladri Hill in Vijayawada, this temple is dedicated to Goddess Kanaka Durga and is one of the most revered Shakti Peethas in South India.

History

The temple is mentioned in ancient texts and associated with Arjuna from the Mahabharata. It has a rich heritage and cultural significance in Andhra Pradesh.

Highlights

Dasara Navaratri celebrations, scenic hilltop view of Krishna River, Pushkar Ghat nearby
