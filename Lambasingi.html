Lambasingi

Often called the 'Kashmir of Andhra Pradesh', Lambasingi is a charming hill station known for its cool climate, misty mornings, and serene landscapes.

Weather & Season

Temperatures can drop close to zero in winter. The best time to visit is from November to January for a chilly experience with scenic foggy hills.

Highlights

Tea & Coffee plantations, Strawberry farms, Kothapalli Waterfalls, Thajangi Reservoir, and early morning mist-covered valleys.

Accommodation

  • Lambasingi Wooden Cottages (₹2,000-₹3,000)
  • Nature Stay Tents (₹1,000-₹1,800)
  • Nearby resorts in Chintapalli (₹1,500-₹4,000)
