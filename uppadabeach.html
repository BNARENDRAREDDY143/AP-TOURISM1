Uppada Beach

Uppada Beach, located near Kakinada in Andhra Pradesh, is known for its wide sandy shore and shallow waters. A serene destination, it's ideal for picnics, family outings, and enjoying natural beauty without the crowd.

Highlights

Peaceful environment, fishing villages nearby, scenic sunrise and sunset views, long coastline with walkable shores.
